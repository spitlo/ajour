  • Source: Writing - rachelbythebay

  • Reading time: 1 min
  • Words 160

I guess I've turned into a collector of numbers that break things sometimes. Here's another one I just heard about, and it's directly related to what just happened with the calendar. The last value for last year is December 31, 2021 at 23:59, and in this format it becomes 2112312359. That just fits into a signed 32 bit number (which tops out at 2147483647).
